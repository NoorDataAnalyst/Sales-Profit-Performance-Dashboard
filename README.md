# Sales & Profit Performance Dashboard 📊

An interactive, end-to-end sales and profitability analysis built using **Advanced Excel** and **Pivot Tables**. This project transforms over **8,300 lines of raw retail transaction data** into a dynamic, decision-ready dashboard to identify revenue drivers, uncover profit leaks, and provide actionable business recommendations.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Features
* **Data Engine:** Cleaned and structured raw transactional data using Excel functions.
* **Pivot Tables & Fields:** Built dynamic aggregations to segment regional, temporal, and category performance.
* **Interactive Dashboard:** Designed an executive-level interface utilizing synchronized slicers (by Year, Category, and State) and custom charts for on-the-fly filtering.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

Our high-level numbers show strong performance: **$1.93M in Sales** with a healthy **12.86% average profit margin ($248K profit)**. However, a deeper look reveals major operational bottlenecks:

### 1. Product & Category Disparities
* **High-Performing Verticals:** **Technology** and **Office Supplies** are the business engines, operating at excellent margins of **17.3%** and **17.9%** respectively.
* **The Furniture Margin Drag:** Despite driving a massive **$622K in sales**, the Furniture category yielded a meager **2.87% profit margin ($17.8K)**.
* **Critical Profit Leaks:** The **Tables** sub-category is our biggest drain, losing over **$12.3K** despite pulling in $167K in sales. **Bookcases** are also operating at a net loss.

### 2. Geographical Performance
* **Powerhouse States:** **California** ($63K profit) and **New York** ($57K profit) dominate profitability.
* **Underperforming States:** Operations in **Texas** (-$21K loss), **Ohio** (-$15.9K loss), and **Pennsylvania** (-$11.8K loss) are heavily eating into our net margins.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

Based on the data-driven findings, the following tactical moves are recommended:

* 📉 **Fix the Profit Leaks:** Investigate and negotiate high shipping, distribution, and supplier costs associated with **Tables** and **Bookcases** to pull their margins back into the green.
* 📍 **Re-evaluate Strategy in Loss-Making States:** Limit aggressive promotional discounting and adjust localized pricing structures in high-cost states like **Texas** and **Ohio**.
* 🎯 **Double Down on Winners:** Reallocate marketing budget and physical inventory toward high-margin sub-categories (like **Copiers** and **Phones**) and highly profitable regions.
